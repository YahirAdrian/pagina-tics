Universidad Autónoma del Estado de Quintana Roo

The Autonomous University of the State of Quintana Roo (UAEQROO) is the main public university in the state of Quintana Roo, with headquarters in the city of Chetumal as well as academic units on the island of Cozumel, the city of Playa del Carmen and in Cancun. It offers 16 degrees at the Chetumal Academic Unit, 5 at the Cozumel Academic Unit, 4 at the Playa del Carmen Academic Unit, and 4 at the Cancun Academic Unit.2
